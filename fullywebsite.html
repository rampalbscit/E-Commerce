Help Center

Order Issues

How can I track my order?
You can track your order from the 'Track Order' section on the website or mobile app. You will also receive regular updates via email and SMS.
What should I do if I received a damaged product?
If you receive a damaged product, please contact our customer support within 24 hours of delivery. We will arrange for a replacement or refund.

Returns & Refunds

What is your return policy?
We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Products must be in original condition with all tags attached. Some items like innerwear, personalized products, and perishables are not returnable.
How long does it take to process a refund?
Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the returned product. The time it takes for the refund to reflect in your account depends on your bank's processing time.

Delivery & Shipping

What are the delivery charges?
We offer free delivery on orders above ₹499. For orders below ₹499, a delivery charge of ₹40 is applicable.
How long does delivery take?
Delivery usually takes 3-7 business days depending on your location. You will receive regular updates about your order status.
